# Welcome to PettahPro

PettahPro is cloud accounting and business operations software, built specifically for Sri Lankan small and medium businesses. It bundles the things you usually buy in five separate pieces — accounting, invoicing, inventory, payroll, and a customer portal — into one system, with all the local statutory requirements (VAT, SSCL, EPF, ETF, PAYE, WHT) ready out of the box.

This site is your **product manual**. If you're trying to figure out how to do something in PettahPro, you should be able to find the answer here.

## Quick links

- **New here?** Start with [Getting started](./getting-started.md) — a 10-minute tour from signup to your first posted invoice.
- **Don't recognise a term?** The [glossary](./concepts/glossary.md) explains every Sri Lankan tax acronym (SSCL, EPF, ETF, PAYE, WHT) and accounting term (DR/CR, AR/AP, FY) we use.

## How each module page is written

Every module page follows the same shape so once you've read one you can scan any other one quickly:

1. **What it does** — one paragraph on what the module is and why you'd use it.
2. **Walkthrough** — the happy path, click by click.
3. **Common tasks** — short recipes for the things people actually need to do.
4. **What gets posted** — what hits your books when you save the document. Useful for accountants reviewing the system.
5. **FAQ** — the questions that tend to come up after the first week.
6. **Related** — links to the other features that connect in.

:::note Built for Sri Lankan businesses

PettahPro is purpose-built for Sri Lanka, and that shows up everywhere:

- **VAT 18%, SSCL 2.5%, WHT 5%/10%, EPF 8%/12%, ETF 3%, PAYE** are all built in — you don't have to configure them.
- **LKR** is your books currency. You can invoice in any currency, but the ledger stays in LKR (which is what Inland Revenue requires).
- **Local payment methods** — cheque, LankaQR, FriMi, Genie are all first-class options on customer payments.

If you're outside Sri Lanka, much of this still works, but the tax defaults will need to change.

:::

:::note Your data is private

Every business on PettahPro is isolated from every other business. Your customers, invoices, employees, and reports are visible only to people you invite to your account. We use database-level isolation, not just app-level filters — so even an internal bug couldn't expose your data to another business.

:::
